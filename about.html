About Us

In 2013 we went on a mission in Addis Ababa with the holy blessing of the Rebbe Shlita of Lubavitch, the Messianic King . Rabbi Eliyahu Habib and Rebbetzin Deborah Leah Rabbi Eliyahu Habib and Rebbetzin Deborah Leah Embarking on a mission was extremely challenging. We went out with little Moshka, who was a year old, to a place empty of Jewish content. And with the help of the name we have restored the local community and BH it can be said that now Judaism is thriving here . Our activities are extensive, the Chabad House provides an answer in almost every field, we have classes, prayers, certifications, an active mikveh, Shabbat and holiday meals. Also, kosher food, a kindergarten, a synagogue at the airport, activities on holidays and of course, we give Emergency response for anyone who needs it Our activities appeal to the local Jewish community, visitors, embassy staff, Israeli travelers and Jews from around the world
